<p><p>The viscosity improvement agent market includes three primary types: polymethacrylate, olefin copolymer, and polyisobutylene. Polymethacrylate offers excellent temperature stability and low shear sensitivity, making it ideal for high-performance lubricants. Olefin copolymers provide enhanced viscosity index and improved low-temperature flow characteristics, benefitting various applications. Polyisobutylene, known for its high viscosity and resistance to oxidation, is commonly used in automotive and industrial lubricants. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of fluids across various industries.</p></p>
