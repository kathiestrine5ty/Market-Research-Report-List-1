<p><strong>The Polyester Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Polyester Coating</li><li>Solvent-Based Polyester Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester coatings market is divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based polyester coatings. Water-based polyester coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering an eco-friendly solution with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them suitable for indoor applications. Conversely, solvent-based polyester coatings use organic solvents, providing excellent durability and finish, typically preferred for outdoor and industrial applications. Each type caters to different industry needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application requirements.</p></p>
