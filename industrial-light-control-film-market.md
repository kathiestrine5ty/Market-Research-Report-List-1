<p><p>The Industrial Light Control Film market is divided into two main types based on substrate materials: PET substrate and Non-PET substrate. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) substrate films are widely used for their durability, lightweight properties, and excellent optical performance, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, Non-PET substrates encompass materials like polycarbonate or acrylic, which may offer unique characteristics such as enhanced impact resistance or specific light-filtering properties. Each type caters to different industry needs and preferences in light management.</p></p>
