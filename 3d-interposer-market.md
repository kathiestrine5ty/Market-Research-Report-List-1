<p><p>The 3D interposer market is categorized into three types: silicon, organic, and glass. Silicon interposers offer high performance and excellent thermal management, making them suitable for advanced packaging solutions in high-density applications. Organic interposers are lighter and more cost-effective, often used in consumer electronics where flexibility and reduced weight are essential. Glass interposers provide superior electrical performance and isolation, ideal for high-frequency applications. Each type supports the evolving demands of miniaturization and enhanced functionality in electronic devices.</p></p>
