<p><p>The machine vision camera market mainly consists of two types: PC systems and smart camera systems. PC systems use powerful computers to process images and perform complex analysis, making them suitable for high-performance applications. They require external hardware and are often used in industrial settings. In contrast, smart camera systems integrate processing capabilities directly into the camera, allowing for real-time image analysis and simpler deployment. These are ideal for applications where space and processing speed are critical.</p></p>
