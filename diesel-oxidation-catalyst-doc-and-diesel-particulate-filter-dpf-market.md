<p><p>The Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) and Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) are crucial components in diesel engine management systems aimed at reducing emissions. The DOC facilitates the oxidation of carbon monoxide and unburned hydrocarbons into harmless carbon dioxide and water, while the DPF captures and stores soot particles from the exhaust, preventing them from being released into the atmosphere. Together, these technologies enhance compliance with stringent emissions regulations and contribute to improved air quality.</p><p>Market growth for DOC and DPF is driven by increasing environmental awareness and additional regulations aimed at reducing vehicular emissions.
