<p><p>High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) lipoplasty devices are categorized into two main types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided systems. Ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to target fatty tissue, ensuring precision during fat reduction treatments. In contrast, MR-guided HIFU systems leverage magnetic resonance imaging, offering enhanced visualization of deeper tissues and structures, which is particularly useful for complex treatment areas. Both types focus energy to selectively destroy fat cells, promoting body contouring with minimal invasiveness.</p></p>
