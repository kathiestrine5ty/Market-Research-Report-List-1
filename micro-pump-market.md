<p><ul><li>Mechanical Micro Pump</li><li>Non-Mechanical Micro Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro pump market is divided into two main types: mechanical and non-mechanical micro pumps. Mechanical micro pumps utilize moving parts to create fluid displacement, providing precise control over flow rates and pressures, making them ideal for applications in medical devices and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, non-mechanical micro pumps operate using principles like fluid pressure or electrokinetic effects, offering advantages such as smaller size and reduced maintenance needs. Both types cater to various industries, emphasizing efficiency and miniaturization in fluid management.</p></p>
