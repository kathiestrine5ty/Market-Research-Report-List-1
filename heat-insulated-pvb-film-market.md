<p><ul><li>Endothermic Film</li><li>Reflective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat insulated PVB film market comprises two main types: endothermic film and reflective film. Endothermic films absorb heat, reducing internal temperatures by converting thermal energy into less harmful forms. These films are ideal for energy-efficient buildings and vehicles. Reflective films, on the other hand, work by reflecting sunlight away, minimizing heat gain and enhancing interior comfort. Both types are increasingly utilized in construction and automotive industries to improve energy efficiency and comfort while reducing energy costs and environmental impact.</p></p>
