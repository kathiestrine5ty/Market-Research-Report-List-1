<p><strong>The Subsea Pumping System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Helical Axial Flow Type Pumping System</li><li>Twin Screw Type Pumping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea pumping system market comprises primarily two types: Helical Axial Flow Pumping Systems and Twin Screw Pumping Systems. Helical Axial Flow Pumps utilize a helical rotor to move fluids along the axis, offering high efficiency for transporting large volumes of fluids at lower pressure. In contrast, Twin Screw Pumps feature two intermeshing screws that provide positive displacement for moving viscous fluids with consistent flow rates and high reliability. Both systems are essential for enhancing oil and gas extraction from underwater reserves.</p></p>
