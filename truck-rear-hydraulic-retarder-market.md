<p><ul><li>Parallel Hydraulic Retarder</li><li>Tandem Hydraulic Retarder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck rear hydraulic retarder market features two primary types: parallel hydraulic retarders and tandem hydraulic retarders. Parallel hydraulic retarders operate alongside the vehicle's braking system, providing additional slowing power without significantly impacting performance. In contrast, tandem hydraulic retarders utilize a series of interconnected hydraulic units to enhance braking efficiency further, enabling better control and reduced wear on traditional brakes. Both types are designed to improve safety and performance in heavy-duty trucks, offering effective solutions for managing speed and stability.</p></p>
