<p><ul><li>Biomembrane</li><li>Synthetic Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-permeable film market consists of two primary types: biomembranes and synthetic membranes. Biomembranes are naturally derived membranes, often sourced from biological materials, and used in various applications such as filtration and drug delivery due to their biocompatibility. Synthetic membranes, on the other hand, are artificially created using polymers, offering customizable properties for diverse applications like water purification and food processing. Both types play crucial roles in industries ranging from pharmaceuticals to environmental management, each leveraging specific characteristics to address unique challenges.</p></p>
