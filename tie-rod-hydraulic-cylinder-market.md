<p><p>The Tie-Rod Hydraulic Cylinder market encompasses three main types: Single Acting, Double Acting, and Multi-Stage/Tandem. Single Acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure in one direction, relying on a spring or gravity for retraction. Double Acting cylinders provide hydraulic force in both extension and retraction, making them versatile for various applications. Multi-Stage/Tandem cylinders, featuring multiple stages, offer higher force and compact design, ideal for applications requiring significant lift or long strokes. Each type serves specific operational needs across industries.</p></p>
