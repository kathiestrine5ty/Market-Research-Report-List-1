<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection System market is divided into two main types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging detects naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, making it suitable for applications like security screening and material analysis without requiring external sources. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate the target, allowing for detailed material characterization and defect detection. Both methods are valuable for non-destructive testing and quality control across various industries.</p></p>
