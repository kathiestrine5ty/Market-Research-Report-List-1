ESCs are derived from early embryos and hold the potential for diverse tissue regeneration. MSCs are sourced from adult tissues and contribute to repair and immune modulation. iPSCs are generated from somatic cells, reprogrammed to an embryonic-like state, offering versatility in research and therapeutic applications. Each type plays a vital role in regenerative medicine and research advances.</p></p>
