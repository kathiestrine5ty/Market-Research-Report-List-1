<p><p>Cyclone desanders come in two primary market types: monoblock and multi-body parallel systems. Monoblock desanders feature a single, compact unit designed for efficient separation of solids from fluids, ideal for smaller applications with limited space. In contrast, multi-body parallel systems consist of multiple desander units working together, offering increased capacity and flexibility for larger projects. These configurations allow for scalability and consistent performance in handling varying flow rates and solid loads, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
