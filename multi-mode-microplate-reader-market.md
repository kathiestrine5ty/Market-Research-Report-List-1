<p><p>The multi-mode microplate reader market encompasses three primary types: absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence. Absorbance readers measure the amount of light absorbed by a sample, making them suitable for quantifying concentrations of colored compounds. Fluorescence readers detect emitted light from samples after excitation, ideal for assays involving fluorescent dyes or proteins. Luminescence readers measure light produced by chemical reactions, often used in enzyme-linked assays and bioluminescence studies. Collectively, these technologies serve diverse applications in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
