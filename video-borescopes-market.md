<p><p>The video borescope market is categorized into flexible and rigid borescopes. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, consist of a straight, inflexible tube with high image quality, suitable for applications requiring precise alignment, such as manufacturing and medical procedures. Both types play a crucial role in non-destructive testing and maintenance.</p></p>
