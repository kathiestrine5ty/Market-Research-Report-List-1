<p><ul><li>Semi-Automated Terminals</li><li>Fully Automated Terminals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Container Terminal market is divided into two main types: Semi-Automated Terminals and Fully Automated Terminals. Semi-Automated Terminals integrate automated equipment like quay cranes and automated guided vehicles (AGVs) while still requiring human operators for certain tasks, offering a balance of efficiency and control. In contrast, Fully Automated Terminals utilize advanced robotics and sophisticated software systems to operate independently, minimizing human intervention and maximizing throughput, safety, and operational efficiency in container handling and logistics.</p></p>
