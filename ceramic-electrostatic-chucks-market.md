<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Electrostatic Chucks market features primarily two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks operate on the principle of electrostatic attraction, providing strong gripping forces with minimal surface contact, enhancing wafer stability during processing. JR Type Electrostatic Chucks utilize a combination of electric field and air pressure effects, offering precise control over the positioning of substrates. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for effective wafer handling and processing efficiency.</p></p>
