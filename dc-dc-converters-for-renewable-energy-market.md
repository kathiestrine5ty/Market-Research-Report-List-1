<p><p>DC-DC converters are essential components in renewable energy systems, allowing for efficient voltage regulation and power management. The market can be divided into two main types: isolated and non-isolated converters. Isolated converters provide electrical separation between input and output, enhancing safety and noise immunity, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. Non-isolated converters, on the other hand, offer simpler designs with higher efficiency for applications where isolation is not a concern, often used in low-voltage, cost-sensitive scenarios.</p></p>
