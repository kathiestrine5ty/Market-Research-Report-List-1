<p><p>IoT antennas in electronic devices are critical for wireless communication, classified into several types. Chip antennas are compact and ideal for space-constrained applications. Wire antennas offer flexibility and better range but require more space. Whip antennas are longer, providing improved signal strength and are commonly used in mobile devices. Together, these antennas support the diverse needs of IoT connectivity.</p></p>
