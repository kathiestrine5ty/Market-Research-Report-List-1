<p><p>The dry honey market includes granulated and powdered honey, both valued for their convenience and extended shelf life. Granulated honey typically retains its natural sweetness while providing a crystalline texture, making it ideal for baking and sweetening beverages. Powdered honey, on the other hand, is produced by drying liquid honey and grinding it into a fine powder, suitable for food products, seasoning, and snacks. Both forms offer versatile uses in culinary applications while maintaining honey's unique flavor profile.</p></p>
