<p><p>The plastic pellet dryers market is segmented into batch and continuous types. Batch dryers operate by processing specific quantities of pellets in cycles, allowing for flexibility in production and easy maintenance. In contrast, continuous dryers provide a steady flow of material, facilitating uninterrupted processing and higher efficiency for large-scale operations. Each type caters to different production needs, where batch dryers are suitable for varied or small-scale operations, while continuous dryers are ideal for consistent, high-volume production settings.</p></p>
