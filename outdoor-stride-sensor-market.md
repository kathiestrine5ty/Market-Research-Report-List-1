<p><ul><li>Gravity Induced</li><li>Vibration Induced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Outdoor Stride Sensor market is divided into two primary types: Gravity Induced and Vibration Induced sensors. Gravity Induced sensors utilize gravitational forces to detect strides and measure movement, making them suitable for applications like fitness tracking and outdoor activities. In contrast, Vibration Induced sensors rely on detecting vibrations generated during movement, providing enhanced accuracy in various terrains. Both types cater to outdoor enthusiasts by improving data accuracy for performance tracking and enhancing user experience in real-time.</p></p>
