<p><p>The photosensitive glass market is primarily divided into two types: transparent glass and opacified glass. In contrast, opacified glass is designed to limit light transmission while offering specific functional benefits, such as reduced glare or enhanced privacy. Both types have distinct uses in industries like construction, electronics, and art, catering to varying aesthetic and functional needs.</p></p>
