<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen Microcatheters</li><li>Dual-Lumen Microcatheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Delivery Microcatheter market is primarily categorized into two types: Single-Lumen Microcatheters and Dual-Lumen Microcatheters. Single-Lumen Microcatheters feature one channel for fluid delivery, facilitating straightforward procedures in various vascular interventions. In contrast, Dual-Lumen Microcatheters possess two separate channels, allowing for simultaneous access for multiple functionalities, such as fluid delivery and pressure monitoring. This versatility makes Dual-Lumen Microcatheters particularly beneficial in complex or time-sensitive medical scenarios, enhancing procedural efficiency and safety in interventional therapies.</p></p>
