<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) enhances vehicle handling and fuel efficiency by replacing traditional hydraulic systems with electric actuators. The market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. C-EPS (Column-mounted EPS) integrates the electric motor into the steering column, providing compact design and improved responsiveness.</p><p>2. P-EPS (Pinion-mounted EPS) positions the motor at the pinion gear, enhancing efficiency and steering feel.</p><p>3. R-EPS (Rack-mounted EPS) directly connects the motor to the steering rack, offering superior control and customization for various driving conditions.</p></p>
