Extended Range Electric Vehicles combine electric power with a gasoline generator, extending their range beyond pure electric capabilities. Hybrid Electric Vehicles integrate both an internal combustion engine with electric propulsion, improving fuel efficiency. Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles generate electricity through hydrogen and oxygen, producing only water as a byproduct. Hydrogen Powered Vehicles specifically use hydrogen as fuel for propulsion, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
