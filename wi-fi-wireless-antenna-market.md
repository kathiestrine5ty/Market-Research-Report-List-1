<p><p>The Wi-Fi wireless antenna market consists of two main types: omni-directional and directional antennas. Omni-directional antennas emit signals in a full 360-degree pattern, making them ideal for providing broad coverage in open spaces, such as home or office environments. In contrast, directional antennas concentrate the signal in a specific direction, enhancing range and performance over greater distances, making them suitable for point-to-point connections or targeted coverage in larger areas. Both types serve distinct needs in wireless communication.</p></p>
