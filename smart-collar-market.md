<p><p>The smart collar market is primarily categorized into GPS-based and radio-based systems. GPS-based collars utilize global positioning satellites for accurate location tracking, making them ideal for outdoor use and vast areas, enabling pet owners to monitor their pets in real-time. In contrast, radio-based collars use short-range radio frequencies, providing reliable tracking within a limited range, often suitable for activities like training and local monitoring. Both types enhance pet safety and owner convenience through advanced technology.</p></p>
