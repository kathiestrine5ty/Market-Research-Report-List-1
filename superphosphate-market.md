<p><ul><li>Normal superphosphate or single superphosphate</li><li>Concentrated or triple superphosphate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The superphosphate market comprises two main types: normal superphosphate (also known as single superphosphate) and concentrated superphosphate (triple superphosphate). Normal superphosphate contains 16-20% phosphorus and is commonly used for general soil enrichment. In contrast, triple superphosphate is more concentrated, containing 42-48% phosphorus, making it more effective for high-demand crops. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, with normal superphosphate suitable for a wide range of applications and triple superphosphate preferred for targeted nutrient management in intensive farming.</p></p>
