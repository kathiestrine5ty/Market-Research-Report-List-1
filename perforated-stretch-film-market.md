<p><p>The perforated stretch film market is categorized into two main types: perforated manual film and perforated machine film. Perforated manual film is designed for hand application, allowing users to wrap products easily while facilitating airflow, which helps reduce moisture buildup. In contrast, perforated machine film is specifically engineered for automated wrapping machines, providing consistency and efficiency in packaging operations. Both types serve common purposes in securing goods during transit while offering ventilation to preserve freshness.</p></p>
