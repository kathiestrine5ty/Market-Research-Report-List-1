<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Simple Matrix</li><li>Active Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large-area LCD display market comprises three primary types: static, simple matrix, and active matrix. Static displays utilize a fixed image, making them suitable for signage. Simple matrix displays use a limited grid of pixels, allowing for basic visual outputs and simplicity in design. Active matrix displays, however, employ a grid of individually controlled pixels, enabling high-resolution images and faster refresh rates. This technology is ideal for applications requiring dynamic visuals, such as TVs and computer monitors.</p></p>
