<p><p>Kaolin powder, used in personal care products, is categorized into hard kaolin and soft kaolin based on its properties. Hard kaolin is a coarser grade, often utilized in formulations for its absorbent and oil-control qualities, making it suitable for facial masks and powders. In contrast, soft kaolin is finer and gentler, ideal for sensitive skin applications and products like talcs and baby powders. Both types enhance product texture, provide thickening, and deliver skin-soothing benefits.</p></p>
