<p><strong>The Paint Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rheology Modification</li><li>Biocides</li><li>Anti-Foaming</li><li>Wetting & Dispersion</li><li>Impact Modification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint additives market encompasses various types that enhance paint performance. Rheology modifiers adjust the flow and viscosity, ensuring smooth application. Biocides protect against microbial growth, extending the paint's life. Anti-foaming agents eliminate foam during mixing and application, improving texture. Wetting and dispersion additives enhance pigment distribution, ensuring uniform color and coverage. Impact modifiers increase the durability and flexibility of paint films, reducing the risk of cracking and chipping. Together, these additives optimize paint quality and longevity.</p></p>
