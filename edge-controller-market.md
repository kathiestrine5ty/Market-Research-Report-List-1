<p><p>The Edge Controller market comprises two primary types: DIN-rail and panel mount. DIN-rail controllers are compact and designed for easy installation in standard electrical cabinets, making them ideal for industrial automation and control systems. In contrast, panel mount controllers are designed to be integrated into larger enclosures or control panels, providing enhanced interface and accessibility for operators. Both types serve critical roles in edge computing, facilitating real-time data processing and device management in various settings, including manufacturing and smart infrastructure.</p></p>
