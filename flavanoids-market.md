<p><p>The flavonoids market comprises various types, each with unique properties and applications. Anthoxanthins include colorless flavonoids, primarily found in fruits and vegetables. Flavanones exhibit antioxidant benefits, often found in citrus fruits. Flavanonols offer similar benefits with added stability in food products. Flavans, or flavan-3-ols, are known for their cardiovascular health benefits. Anthocyanidins are responsible for vibrant pigmentation in berries and red cabbage. Isoflavonoids, commonly derived from soy, are associated with hormonal health. Other flavonoids encompass various lesser-known compounds, contributing to diverse market applications.</p></p>
