<p><p>Molecular diagnostics in infectious disease testing encompasses various techniques for detecting pathogens. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences for accurate detection. Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) enables rapid amplification at a constant temperature. Microarrays allow simultaneous analysis of multiple pathogens using probes on a single chip. Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) provides comprehensive genomic data, facilitating pathogen identification and characterization. Hybridization methods involve specific binding of nucleic acids for detection, while "Others" includes emerging or niche technologies in the field.</p></p>
