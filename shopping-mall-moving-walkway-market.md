<p><p>Shopping mall moving walkways can be categorized into three main types: parallel, multi-parallel, and others. </p><p>Parallel walkways consist of a single pathway, facilitating efficient movement between points in a straight line. Multi-parallel walkways feature multiple lanes operating side by side, enabling higher pedestrian throughput. Other types may include curved or multi-directional walkways, offering flexibility in navigating complex mall layouts. Each type enhances shopper convenience, reduces fatigue, and encourages exploration of retail spaces within the shopping environment.</p></p>
