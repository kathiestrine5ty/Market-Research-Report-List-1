<p><p>The silicon-based photodetector market encompasses various technologies, notably Silicon Drift Detectors (SDDs) and Silicon Photomultipliers (SiPMs). SDDs are known for their high energy resolution and low noise, making them ideal for X-ray spectroscopy and particle detection. In contrast, SiPMs offer high gain and excellent timing resolution, ideal for applications like medical imaging and particle physics. Both types support advancements in detection sensitivity and accuracy, driving growth across sectors such as healthcare, security, and scientific research.</p></p>
