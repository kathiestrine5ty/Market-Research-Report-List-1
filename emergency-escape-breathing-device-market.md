<p><p>The Emergency Escape Breathing Device (EEBD) market is primarily categorized into two types: Air Supplying Breathing Apparatus and Air Purifying Breathing Apparatus. Air Supplying Breathing Apparatus provides a direct supply of breathable air from a tank, suitable for environments with toxic gases or limited oxygen. In contrast, Air Purifying Breathing Apparatus filters and cleans ambient air, removing harmful substances while allowing the user to breathe. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in hazardous situations, particularly in industrial and emergency settings.</p></p>
