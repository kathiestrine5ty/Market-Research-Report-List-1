<p><p>The breathing machines market encompasses various devices essential for respiratory support. Asthma nebulizers deliver medication directly to the lungs for asthma management. PAP (Positive Airway Pressure) machines provide continuous airflow, aiding those with sleep apnea. Oxygen concentrators extract oxygen from the air, supplying patients with higher concentrations necessary for conditions like COPD. Ventilators assist or control breathing for critically ill patients, ensuring proper oxygenation and carbon dioxide removal during medical emergencies or surgeries. Each type serves vital roles in respiratory care.</p></p>
