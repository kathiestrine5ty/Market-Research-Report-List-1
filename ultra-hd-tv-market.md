<p><p>The Ultra-HD TV market primarily comprises two types: LCD TVs and OLED TVs. LCD TVs utilize liquid crystal technology, offering vibrant colors and high brightness, making them ideal for well-lit environments. However, they may struggle with deep black levels. Conversely, OLED TVs use organic compounds that emit light individually, delivering superior contrast, deep blacks, and wider viewing angles. While OLEDs generally provide a more immersive viewing experience, they are often priced higher than LCDs, appealing to different consumer preferences and budgets.</p></p>
