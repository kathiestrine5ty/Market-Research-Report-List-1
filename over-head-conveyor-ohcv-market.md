<p><p>Overhead conveyors (OHCV) are systems used to transport materials efficiently within industrial settings. The market includes various types, with Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC) designs featuring one camshaft to control valve timing, thus simplifying engine construction and improving efficiency. In contrast, Double Overhead Camshaft (DOHC) systems utilize two camshafts for better airflow and performance at high RPMs, allowing for more precise control of valves. Both types serve distinct operational needs in automotive and manufacturing industries, enhancing productivity and performance.</p></p>
