<p><p>The semi-automatic beverage carton packaging machinery market includes various types designed for efficient packing. Horizontal end side-load machinery employs a lateral loading method for cartons, while top-load machines facilitate vertical insertion. Wraparound technology uses flexible materials to securely encase products, whereas vertical leaflet machines apply labels or promotional materials during the packing process. Vertical sleeve systems use tubular film to provide effective packaging for various beverage types, enhancing shelf appeal while ensuring product protection throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
