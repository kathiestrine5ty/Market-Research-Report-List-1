<p><p>The asphalt sealer market consists of two main types: oil-based and water-based sealers. Oil-based sealers are often favored for their durability and weather resistance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. They provide a rich black finish but have longer drying times and potential environmental concerns. Water-based sealers, on the other hand, are more eco-friendly, easier to apply, and dry quickly, making them ideal for residential projects. Each type caters to specific user needs regarding performance, application, and environmental impact.</p></p>
