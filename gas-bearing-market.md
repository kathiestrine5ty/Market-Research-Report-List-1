<p><strong>The Gas Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerostatic</li><li>Aerodynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas bearing market consists of two main types: aerostatic and aerodynamic bearings. Aerostatic bearings rely on a constant pressurized gas supply, creating a thin film that supports the load without any contact, providing frictionless motion. They are ideal for low-speed applications. In contrast, aerodynamic bearings utilize high-speed airflow generated by the rotating shaft to create lift, making them suitable for high-speed and dynamic applications like turbines and aerospace. Both types enhance performance and reduce wear in various industries.</p></p>
