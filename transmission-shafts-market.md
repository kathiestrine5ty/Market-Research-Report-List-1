<p><p>Transmission shafts are critical components in vehicles and machinery, transferring power between different parts. The **output shaft** delivers power from the transmission system to the wheels or other driven components, enabling motion. The **input shaft** receives power from the engine or motor and transfers it into the transmission system for further distribution. Both shafts are essential for efficient power transmission, with the input shaft initiating the process and the output shaft completing it, ensuring seamless operation in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
