<p><strong>The Wheel Alignment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thrust Alignment</li><li>Two-Wheel Alignment</li><li>Four-Wheel Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel alignment market encompasses three primary types: thrust alignment, two-wheel alignment, and four-wheel alignment. Thrust alignment focuses on aligning the rear wheels to ensure they are parallel to the front wheels, typically for older vehicles with solid rear axles. Two-wheel alignment adjusts the front wheels only, suitable for vehicles with front-wheel drive. In contrast, four-wheel alignment involves adjusting all four wheels, ensuring comprehensive tire performance and vehicle handling, making it the most commonly recommended method for modern vehicles.</p></p>
