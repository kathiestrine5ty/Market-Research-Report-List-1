<p><p>The Zinc Series Soft Ferrite Core market comprises three main types: Manganese-Zinc (Mn-Zn) Ferrite Core, known for its high permeability and suitable for low-frequency applications; Nickel-Zinc (Ni-Zn) Ferrite Core, ideal for high-frequency applications due to its lower losses; and Magnesium-Zinc (Mg-Zn) Ferrite Core, which offers a balance between Mn-Zn and Ni-Zn properties, making it versatile for various electronic components. Each type serves distinct applications in magnetic inductors, transformers, and electromagnetic interference shielding.</p></p>
