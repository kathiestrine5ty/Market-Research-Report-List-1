<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Photonic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz power detectors market is primarily segmented into two types: thermal and photonic. Thermal type detectors operate by measuring temperature changes caused by absorbed terahertz radiation, offering simple design and good sensitivity, but generally slower response times. In contrast, photonic type detectors utilize the interaction of terahertz waves with materials that exhibit nonlinear optical properties, enabling faster response and enhanced performance in specific applications. Both types serve distinct needs in scientific, industrial, and security domains, driving market growth.</p></p>
