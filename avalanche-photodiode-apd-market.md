<p><p>An Avalanche Photodiode (APD) is a specialized semiconductor device that is highly sensitive to light, used for detecting low levels of optical signals. Unlike standard photodiodes, APDs operate on the principle of avalanche multiplication, where incoming photons generate charge carriers that trigger a chain reaction, amplifying the signal significantly. This makes them ideal for applications in fiber optic communications, LiDAR systems, medical imaging, and security systems.</p><p>The Avalanche Photodiode market is experiencing robust growth, fueled by advancements in technology and increasing demand across various sectors.
