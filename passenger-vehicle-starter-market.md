<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle starter market can be categorized into two primary types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starters use a mechanical link to engage the starter motor, providing a straightforward and reliable operation. In contrast, electromagnetic control starters employ an electromagnetic switch to connect the battery to the starter motor, enabling a smoother and more efficient start. Both types are essential for vehicle reliability, yet they cater to different consumer preferences for performance and technology in modern passenger vehicles.</p></p>
