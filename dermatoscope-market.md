<p><ul><li>Traditional Dermatoscope</li><li>Digital Dermatoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dermatoscope market is primarily divided into two types: traditional and digital dermatoscopes. Traditional dermatoscopes utilize optical lenses and light sources to provide a magnified view of skin lesions, aiding in diagnosis and analysis. In contrast, digital dermatoscopes incorporate advanced imaging technology, allowing for high-resolution images and video capture. This enables enhanced documentation, remote consultations, and integration with telemedicine platforms. Both types facilitate improved skin cancer detection and monitoring but differ in their technological capabilities and applications.</p></p>
