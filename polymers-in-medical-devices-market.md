<p><p>The polymers in medical devices market consists of various types, including Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), Polypropylene (PP), Polystyrene (PS), Polyethylene (PE), and Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE). PVC is widely used for its flexibility and durability in products like IV bags. PP offers chemical resistance and is used in syringes and containers. PS is valued for its rigidity in disposable items. PE is known for its low density and is utilized in packaging. TPE combines the properties of rubber and plastic, making it ideal for soft-touch applications. Other polymer types also cater to specific medical needs.</p></p>
