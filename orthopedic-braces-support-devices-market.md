<p><p>The orthopedic braces and support devices market is categorized into three main types: upper-limb orthoses, lower-limb orthoses, and spinal orthoses. Upper-limb orthoses support the arms and shoulders, often used for conditions like fractures or tendon injuries. Lower-limb orthoses aid the legs and feet, addressing issues such as ligament injuries or rehabilitation after surgery. Spinal orthoses focus on the spine, providing support for conditions like scoliosis or post-operative recovery, enhancing mobility and stability for patients.</p></p>
