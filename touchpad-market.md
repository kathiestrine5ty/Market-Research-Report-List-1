<p><p>The touchpad market comprises two primary types: traditional touchpads and buttonless touchpads. Traditional touchpads feature designated physical buttons for clicking, providing tactile feedback that users often prefer. In contrast, buttonless touchpads rely on gesture-based controls, where users tap or swipe directly on the surface for all interactions, enhancing aesthetics and simplifying design. The buttonless versions tend to be more popular in modern devices due to their sleek appearance and support for multi-touch gestures, offering a more seamless user experience.</p></p>
