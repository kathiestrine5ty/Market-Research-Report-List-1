<p><ul><li>Forged</li><li>Cast rods</li><li>Powder Metals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive connecting rod market is categorized into three main types: forged, cast, and powder metal rods. Forged rods are created through deforming heated metal, offering superior strength and durability, making them ideal for high-performance engines. Cast rods are produced by pouring molten metal into molds, providing good strength at a lower cost, commonly used in standard engines. Powder metal rods are made by compacting metal powder, yielding precise shapes and properties, suitable for specific applications where lightweight design is crucial.</p></p>
