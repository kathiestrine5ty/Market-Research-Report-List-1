<p><p>The farm tyre market is primarily segmented into bias tyres and radial tyres. Bias tyres have layers of fabric crisscrossed, providing a sturdy structure and better grip, making them suitable for soft or uneven terrains. They tend to be more affordable but may have heat build-up issues. In contrast, radial tyres feature steel belts beneath the tread, offering superior durability, lower rolling resistance, and enhanced fuel efficiency, making them ideal for high-speed operations on firmer surfaces. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs.</p></p>
