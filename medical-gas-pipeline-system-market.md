<p><p>The Medical Gas Pipeline System market comprises two primary types: centralized and distributed systems. Centralized systems involve a single source, typically a central supply unit, delivering medical gases through pipelines to various hospital areas. This setup enhances efficiency and reduces equipment redundancy. In contrast, distributed systems utilize multiple smaller units spread throughout the facility, allowing for localized distribution. This design offers flexibility and can be beneficial in larger facilities with diverse needs, ensuring each department has immediate access to necessary gases.</p></p>
