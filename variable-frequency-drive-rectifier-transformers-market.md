<p><p>The Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) rectifier transformers market is primarily divided into two types: liquid-filled and dry-type transformers. Liquid-filled transformers utilize insulating oil, providing better cooling and efficiency, making them suitable for larger industrial applications. Conversely, dry-type transformers are air-cooled and do not use liquid insulation, offering advantages in terms of safety and maintenance. They are often preferred in environments where fire hazards are a concern. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring reliable power supply for variable frequency drives.</p></p>
