<p><ul><li>Nickeliferous Limonite</li><li>Garnierite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel ore market primarily consists of nickeliferous limonite and garnierite, both crucial for nickel production. Nickeliferous limonite, formed from weathered ultramafic rocks, is characterized by its high iron and nickel content, often used in nickel laterite processing. Garnierite, a green, serpentine-like mineral, is rich in nickel and has a lower iron content, making it suitable for hydrometallurgical extraction methods. Both types play a vital role in meeting the rising global demand for nickel in various industries.</p></p>
