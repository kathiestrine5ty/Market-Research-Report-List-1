<p><p>The commercial pool cleaning robots market is primarily categorized into two types based on their drive mechanisms: crawler drive and wheel drive. Crawler drive robots utilize tracks for better traction and stability on uneven surfaces, making them suitable for larger and deeper pools. In contrast, wheel drive robots employ wheels for mobility, offering a faster and more agile cleaning experience on flat surfaces. Both types are designed to enhance efficiency and reduce manual labor in maintaining clean, hygienic swimming environments.</p></p>
