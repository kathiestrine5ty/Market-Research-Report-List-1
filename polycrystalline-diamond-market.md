<p><strong>The Polycrystalline Diamond Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PDSs</li><li>PDCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polycrystalline diamond (PCD) market is primarily divided into two types: Polycrystalline Diamond Scraps (PDSs) and Polycrystalline Diamond Compact (PDCs). PDSs consist of diamond particles that are often reclaimed from production processes, making them cost-effective for various applications. PDCs, on the other hand, are engineered tools that combine a layer of diamond with a carbide substrate, utilized for drilling and cutting in industries like oil and gas, mining, and construction due to their durability and efficiency.</p></p>
