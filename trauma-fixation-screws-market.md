<p><p>The Trauma Fixation Screws market consists of three main types: stainless steel, titanium, and bioabsorbable. Stainless steel screws are known for their strength and durability, making them ideal for long-term fixation. Titanium screws offer a lighter weight alternative with excellent biocompatibility, reducing the risk of infection and promoting healing. Bioabsorbable screws are designed to dissolve over time, eliminating the need for a second surgery and minimizing long-term complications. Each type serves specific patient needs and surgical applications.</p></p>
