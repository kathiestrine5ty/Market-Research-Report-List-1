<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) market is divided into two primary types: Rigid Polyvinyl Chloride (RPVC) and Flexible Polyvinyl Chloride (FPVC). RPVC is characterized by its strong, durable structure, making it ideal for construction applications like pipes, window frames, and flooring. In contrast, FPVC incorporates plasticizers, allowing for greater flexibility and softness, which makes it suitable for products like electrical cable insulation, medical devices, and inflatable items. Both types have significant industrial and consumer applications, driven by their unique properties.</p></p>
