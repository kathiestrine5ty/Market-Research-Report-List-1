<p><ul><li>Parallel-axis Helical Gear Units</li><li>Perpendicular-axis Helical Gear Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helical gear units market is primarily divided into two types: parallel-axis and perpendicular-axis helical gear units. Parallel-axis helical gear units feature shafts that run parallel, providing smooth and efficient power transmission, commonly used in industrial applications. Perpendicular-axis helical gear units, on the other hand, have shafts that intersect at right angles, allowing for versatile space-saving designs and enabling changes in torque direction. Both types offer advantages such as high load capacity and reduced noise during operation.</p></p>
