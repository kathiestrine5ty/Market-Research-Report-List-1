<p><p>The Polyethersulfone dialyzers market is categorized into high permeability and low permeability segments, based on the membrane's ability to allow the passage of solutes and fluids. High permeability dialyzers facilitate efficient clearance of larger molecules, enhancing dialysis efficacy and reducing treatment time, making them suitable for patients requiring intensive care. Low permeability dialyzers, on the other hand, are designed for more selective filtration, offering greater control over the removal of specific solutes, and are often used for standard dialysis treatments.</p></p>
