<p><p>The fully automatic voltage regulators (AVRs) market comprises two main types: single-phase and three-phase automatic voltage regulators. Single-phase AVRs are designed for residential and light commercial applications, providing stable voltage for devices powered by a single-phase supply. In contrast, three-phase AVRs cater to industrial and larger commercial sectors, managing voltage for machinery and equipment that requires three-phase power. Both types ensure consistent voltage levels, protect against fluctuations, and enhance the efficiency and lifespan of electrical equipment.</p></p>
