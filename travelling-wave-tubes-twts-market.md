<p><strong>The Travelling wave Tubes TWTs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Helix TWTs</li><li>Coupled Cavity TWTs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Traveling Wave Tube (TWT) market is primarily segmented into two types: Helix TWTs and Coupled Cavity TWTs. Helix TWTs utilize a helix-shaped slow wave structure to amplify signals, offering broad bandwidth and high efficiency, making them suitable for communication satellites and radar systems. In contrast, Coupled Cavity TWTs employ a series of coupled cavities to achieve high gain at specific frequencies, ideal for applications requiring narrowband amplification, such as in microwave and millimeter-wave systems. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
