<p><p>The thermochromic pigments market is categorized into two main types: reversible and irreversible thermochromic pigments. Reversible thermochromic pigments change color with temperature fluctuations but return to their original hue once the temperature stabilizes, making them ideal for applications like temperature indicators and novelty products. In contrast, irreversible thermochromic pigments undergo a permanent color change after exposure to high temperatures or certain conditions, often used in security features and packaging to indicate tampering or exposure.</p></p>
