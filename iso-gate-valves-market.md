<p><p>The ISO gate valves market is primarily categorized into two types: pneumatic and manual valves. Pneumatic gate valves utilize compressed air or gas for actuation, offering rapid and precise control, making them ideal for automated systems in industrial applications. Manual gate valves, on the other hand, require physical effort to operate, typically using a handwheel for control. These valves are widely used in settings where automation is not essential, providing reliability and simplicity for flow regulation in various processes.</p></p>
