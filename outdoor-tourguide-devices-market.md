<p><ul><li>Wire</li><li>Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor tour guide devices market is divided into two main types: wire and wireless. Wired devices rely on physical connections, often using headsets linked to a central audio source, ensuring reliable sound quality but limiting mobility. In contrast, wireless devices utilize Bluetooth or radio frequency technology, allowing for greater freedom of movement and enhanced user experience. While wired options may appeal to traditional users seeking consistent audio, wireless devices cater to modern preferences for convenience and flexibility in outdoor tours.</p></p>
