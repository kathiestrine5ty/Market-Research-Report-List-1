<p><p>Helical Submerged Arc Welding (HSAW) steel pipe is a manufacturing process that produces spiral-welded pipes using submerged arc welding technology. This method involves feeding a flat steel strip into a forming machine where it is rolled into a spiral shape and welded along the seam, resulting in high-quality, long-lasting pipes primarily used in the oil and gas, water supply, and construction industries.</p><p>The market for HSAW steel pipes is anticipated to experience significant growth due to rising infrastructure development, increasing energy demands, and the expansion of the pipeline industry.
