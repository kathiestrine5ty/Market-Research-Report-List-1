<p><p>Ion Beam Based Deposition (IBD) encompasses two key market types: Single Ion Beam Deposition Technology and Dual Ion Beam Deposition Technology. Single Ion Beam Technology utilizes a single ion beam for material deposition, offering precise control and uniform thickness, making it ideal for high-quality thin films. Conversely, Dual Ion Beam Technology employs two ion beams, enhancing film quality and optimizing the deposition process for complex structures. Both technologies cater to diverse applications in electronics, optics, and surface coatings, driving innovation in these sectors.</p></p>
