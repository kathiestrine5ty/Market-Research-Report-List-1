<p><p>The lithium-ion battery separator market is categorized into various types based on the construction of the separators. Single-layer separators, typically made of polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE), offer basic performance. Bilayer separators, combining materials like PP/PE or PP/PP, enhance thermal stability and permeability. Trilayer separators, constructed with a combination of PP/PE/PP, provide superior thermal resistance and mechanical strength, improving safety and efficiency. Each type caters to specific battery applications, addressing the demand for higher energy density and longevity in modern batteries.</p></p>
