<p><p>Film capacitors are essential components in household appliances, categorized into AC and DC types. AC film capacitors are designed to handle alternating current, making them ideal for applications such as motor start and run capacitors in devices like refrigerators and air conditioners. In contrast, DC film capacitors are tailored for direct current applications, commonly used in power supply circuits and signal processing within appliances. Both types enhance energy efficiency and improve the overall performance of household electrical devices.</p></p>
