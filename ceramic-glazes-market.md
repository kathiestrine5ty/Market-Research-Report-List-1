<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Resin Coating</li><li>Water Soluble Resin Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic glazes market can be segmented into solvent-based resin coatings and water-soluble resin coatings. Solvent-based resin coatings utilize organic solvents to dissolve resins, providing a durable finish ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, water-soluble resin coatings rely on water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly advantages and lower VOC emissions. Both types serve specific needs in the ceramics industry, influencing factors like application methods, drying times, and environmental considerations, thus catering to diverse customer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
