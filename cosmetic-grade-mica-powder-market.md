<p><p>The cosmetic grade mica powder market is categorized into wet ground and dry ground types. Wet ground mica is produced using a water-based process, resulting in finer particle sizes and increased purity, making it ideal for high-end cosmetics. Conversely, dry ground mica is created through a grinding process without water, leading to coarser particles and a more cost-effective product. Both types are used for their shimmer and luster in makeup, skincare, and other beauty products, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
