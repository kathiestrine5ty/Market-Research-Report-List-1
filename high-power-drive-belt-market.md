<p><p>The high-power drive belt market is primarily categorized into three types: V belts, flat belts, and other specialized belts. V belts are designed with a trapezoidal cross-section, providing efficient power transmission and minimal slippage, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Flat belts, characterized by their simple design and flexible nature, are commonly used in light to medium duty operations. Other belts encompass unique designs, such as round belts and synchronous belts, catering to specific industrial needs and innovative machinery requirements.</p></p>
