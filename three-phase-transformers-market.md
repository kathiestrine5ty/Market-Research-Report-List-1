<p><p>The three-phase transformer market consists of core-type and shell-type transformers. Core-type transformers feature either a single core or two cores, optimizing space and reducing losses. In contrast, shell-type transformers encase the windings within the core, enhancing mechanical strength and electrical performance. Core-type transformers are favored for their straightforward construction and efficiency, while shell-type designs offer superior protection and reduced electromagnetic interference. Both types cater to diverse applications in industrial, commercial, and utility sectors, facilitating efficient power distribution.</p></p>
