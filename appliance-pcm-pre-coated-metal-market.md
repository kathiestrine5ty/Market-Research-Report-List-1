<p><p>The Appliance PCM (Pre-coated Metal) market comprises various types of coated sheets used in manufacturing appliances. Hot Dip Aluminum Zinc Plates feature a combination of aluminum and zinc for enhanced durability. Galvanized Steel Sheets are sheets coated with zinc to prevent rusting. Cold Rolled Plates are processed at room temperature, offering a smooth finish for high-quality applications, often utilized in home appliances and furniture.</p></p>
