<p><p>The automotive brake adjusters market consists of two main types: manual and automatic brake adjusters. Manual brake adjusters require drivers or technicians to manually adjust the brake system for optimal performance, often leading to more hands-on maintenance. In contrast, automatic brake adjusters continuously monitor and adjust brake performance without user intervention, enhancing safety and convenience. This functionality reduces wear and ensures consistent braking efficiency, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and vehicle applications.</p></p>
