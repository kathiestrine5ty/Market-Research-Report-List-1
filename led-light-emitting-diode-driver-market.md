<p><p>The LED driver market comprises two primary types: constant voltage and constant current drivers. Constant voltage drivers supply a steady voltage level, allowing multiple LEDs to operate effectively within their specified range, suitable for larger installations. In contrast, constant current drivers provide a consistent current output, ensuring uniform brightness and protecting LEDs from fluctuations, making them ideal for applications requiring precise light intensity. Each type is designed to optimize LED performance and longevity while catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
