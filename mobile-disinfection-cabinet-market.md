<p><p>The mobile disinfection cabinet market comprises several key types, each utilizing different technologies for effective pathogen elimination. High-pressure steam disinfection employs steam to penetrate surfaces, killing microorganisms. Electrothermal disinfection uses heated surfaces to eradicate germs. Ozone disinfection leverages ozone gas, which is a powerful oxidizing agent, to disinfect items. Ultraviolet disinfection utilizes UV light to damage the DNA of pathogens, rendering them inactive. Each method offers unique benefits and applications, catering to diverse disinfection needs across various industries.</p></p>
