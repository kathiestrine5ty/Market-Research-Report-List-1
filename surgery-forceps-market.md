<p><p>The surgery forceps market is primarily divided into two types: electrosurgical forceps and normal surgery forceps. Electrosurgical forceps utilize electrical currents for cutting and coagulating tissue, providing precision and reduced bleeding during surgical procedures. Normal surgery forceps, on the other hand, are manually operated instruments used for grasping, holding, and manipulating tissues. Both types serve essential roles in surgery, with electrosurgical forceps offering advanced capabilities for minimally invasive techniques, while normal forceps provide versatility in various surgical tasks.</p></p>
