<p><strong>The Boat Electric Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaftline Propulsion</li><li>Pod Propulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat electric propulsion systems are primarily classified into two types: shaftline propulsion and pod propulsion. Shaftline propulsion involves a traditional design where an electric motor drives a shaft connected to the propeller, providing a reliable and efficient means of movement. In contrast, pod propulsion features a pod-mounted electric motor that can rotate 360 degrees, offering enhanced maneuverability and flexibility. Both systems cater to varying needs in marine applications, emphasizing efficiency and performance in sustainable boating.</p></p>
