<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital temperature and humidity sensor market can be categorized into two primary types: analog output and digital output. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals that correspond to temperature and humidity levels, making them suitable for simple applications and integration with basic data acquisition systems. In contrast, digital output sensors convert readings into binary data, offering higher accuracy, ease of integration with microcontrollers, and advanced features like calibration and compensation, making them ideal for modern automation and IoT applications.</p></p>
