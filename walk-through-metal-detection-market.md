<p><ul><li>LC Oscillating Metal Detector</li><li>Pulse Induction (PI) Metal Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The walk-through metal detection market features two primary types: LC Oscillating Metal Detectors and Pulse Induction (PI) Metal Detectors. LC Oscillating Metal Detectors operate using alternating current to create electromagnetic fields, detecting ferrous and non-ferrous metals effectively, making them ideal for security screening. In contrast, Pulse Induction Metal Detectors utilize short bursts of current to generate magnetic fields, providing deeper penetration and sensitivity for detecting metals, particularly in challenging environments. Together, these technologies enhance security and detection capabilities across various applications.</p></p>
