<p><p>Thin Film Evaporators (TFE) are advanced thermal separation equipment used to concentrate liquid product streams by evaporating solvents under controlled conditions. The market comprises two main types: vertical and horizontal thin film evaporators. Vertical TFE systems utilize a vertical orientation, allowing efficient gravity-assisted film formation, ideal for high-viscosity liquids. In contrast, horizontal TFEs operate with a horizontal layout, optimizing heat transfer and scalability, making them suitable for large volume processes and diverse applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and chemical industries.</p></p>
