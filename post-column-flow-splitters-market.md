<p><p>Post-column flow splitters are essential in chromatography for dividing the effluent from the column into multiple paths. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Double Fixed Splitters** maintain a consistent ratio across two outlets, ideal for multiple detector setups.</p><p>2. **Replaceable Fixed Splitters** allow users to change the splitting ratio by swapping out components, offering flexibility.</p><p>3. **Adjustable Splitters** enable real-time modifications to the flow ratio, catering to varying analytical requirements and optimizing detector performance.</p></p>
