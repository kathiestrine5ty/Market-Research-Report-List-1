<p><p>Feed additives for the aquaculture market are essential for enhancing growth, health, and feed efficiency in aquatic species. They are categorized into two main types: particle and liquid. Particle additives are solid forms that are mixed into feed pellets, providing nutrients, probiotics, or medications that improve digestibility and immunity. Liquid additives, on the other hand, are typically emulsified or solutions that can be easily dispersed in water, allowing for quick absorption and targeted delivery of vital nutrients directly into the aquatic environment.</p></p>
