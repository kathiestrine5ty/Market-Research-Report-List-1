These catheters allow for prolonged intravenous therapy while minimizing the risk of complications associated with traditional IVs. The demand for PICCs is driven by the rising incidence of preterm births and the increasing complexity of neonatal care.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates a significant expansion in the Neonatal PICCs market, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 11.80% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Neonatal Peripherally Inserted Central Venous Catheter (PICC) market is divided into powered and conventional types.
