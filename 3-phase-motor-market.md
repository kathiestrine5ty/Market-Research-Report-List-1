<p><p>The 3-phase motor market primarily includes synchronous and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed, synchronized with the supply frequency, making them ideal for applications requiring precision. They are commonly used in large industrial machines. In contrast, induction motors rely on electromagnetic induction and are widely favored for their ruggedness and simplicity. They typically operate at speeds slightly below the synchronous speed, making them suitable for various applications, including pumps and fans due to their durability and efficiency.</p></p>
