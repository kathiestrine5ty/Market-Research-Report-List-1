<p><p>The intumescent acrylic sealant market is classified into two primary types: water-based and solvent-based sealants. Water-based sealants are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and have low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, making them suitable for indoor applications. Solvent-based sealants, on the other hand, provide superior adhesion and durability, making them ideal for outdoor use and harsher environments. Both types effectively expand when exposed to heat, forming a protective barrier against fire, illustrating their essential roles in fire safety applications.</p></p>
