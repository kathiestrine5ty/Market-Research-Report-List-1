<p><p>The Portable Medical Vacuum System market is segmented into three primary technologies: Dry Claw Pump Technology, Dry Rotary Vane Technology, and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology. </p><p>Dry Claw Pump Technology operates without lubricants, ensuring clean and dry vacuum conditions vital for medical applications. Dry Rotary Vane Technology utilizes rotating vanes in a vacuum chamber, also maintaining an oil-free environment. Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology relies on oil for lubrication, offering higher efficiency but necessitating careful management to avoid contamination. Each technology serves specific healthcare requirements, balancing performance and hygiene.</p></p>
