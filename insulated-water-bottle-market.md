<p><p>The insulated water bottle market primarily consists of two types: non-vacuum stainless steel and vacuum stainless steel insulated bottles. Non-vacuum bottles use double-wall construction without a vacuum layer, providing moderate insulation and typically lower cost. In contrast, vacuum stainless steel bottles are designed with an airless space between walls, offering superior insulation that keeps beverages hot or cold for extended periods. This advanced technology attracts consumers seeking optimal temperature retention for hydration during various activities.</p></p>
