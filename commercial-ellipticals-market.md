<p><ul><li>Flywheel Front Ellipticals</li><li>Flywheel Rear Ellipticals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial ellipticals market features two primary types: flywheel front ellipticals and flywheel rear ellipticals. Flywheel front ellipticals position the flywheel at the front of the machine, offering a more natural stride and greater stability, ideal for heavier usage in commercial settings. Conversely, flywheel rear ellipticals have the flywheel at the back, providing a smoother motion and compact design, making them suitable for smaller spaces. Both types cater to different user preferences and workout experiences in fitness facilities.</p></p>
