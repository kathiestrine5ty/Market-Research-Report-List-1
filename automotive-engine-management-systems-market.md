<p><p>Automotive Engine Management Systems encompass various technologies to optimize engine performance and efficiency. MPFi (Multi-Point Fuel Injection) systems inject fuel into each cylinder for precise delivery, enhancing power and reducing emissions. GDi (Gasoline Direct Injection) systems inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber, improving fuel efficiency and performance. CRDi (Common Rail Direct Injection) systems optimize diesel engine performance by using a common rail to supply fuel to injectors, enhancing efficiency and reducing noise. Other systems may include hybrid and alternative fuel technologies, contributing to market diversity.</p></p>
