<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Intraoral Dental X-ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Occlusal X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intraoral dental X-ray systems include various types specialized for different diagnostic purposes. Bitewing X-rays capture the upper and lower teeth in one film, ideal for detecting cavities and monitoring interproximal areas. Periapical X-rays focus on one or two teeth, detailing the entire tooth structure and surrounding bone, useful for identifying issues like infections or fractures. Occlusal X-rays provide a broader view of the dental arch, assisting in assessing the overall alignment and positioning of teeth.</p></p>
