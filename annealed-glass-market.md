<p><p>The annealed glass market can be categorized into two main types: inorganic annealed glass and organic annealed glass. Inorganic annealed glass is typically produced using silica-based materials, offering high thermal stability and resistance to chemical corrosion, making it ideal for architectural and industrial applications. In contrast, organic annealed glass incorporates organic compounds, which enhance properties like flexibility and lightweight characteristics, suitable for automotive and consumer products. Both types serve distinct market needs based on their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
