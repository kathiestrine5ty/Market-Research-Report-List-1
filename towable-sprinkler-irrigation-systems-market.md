<p><p>Towable sprinkler irrigation systems encompass several types designed for efficient water distribution in agriculture. Center pivot systems feature rotating arms mounted on wheeled towers, ideal for large fields. Lateral move systems consist of a long pipeline that moves laterally across a field, providing uniform coverage. Solid set systems involve fixed pipes and sprinklers for consistent irrigation. Other types include traveling guns and wheel lines, which offer versatility for different crop needs and field layouts, ensuring optimal water usage and crop health.</p></p>
