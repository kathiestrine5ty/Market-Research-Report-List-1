<p><strong>The Copolyester Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Copolyester Resin</li><li>Thermosetting Copolyester Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copolyester resins market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic copolyester resin and thermosetting copolyester resin. Thermoplastic copolyester resins are characterized by their ability to be reprocessed and reshaped upon heating, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility and durability. In contrast, thermosetting copolyester resins undergo a chemical change during curing, resulting in a hardened structure that cannot be remolded. Each type serves distinct applications across industries such as packaging, automotive, and textiles, leveraging unique properties for specific needs.</p></p>
