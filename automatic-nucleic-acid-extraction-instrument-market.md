<p><p>The Automatic Nucleic Acid Extraction Instrument market comprises two primary methods: Spin Column Method and Magnetic Bead Method. The Spin Column Method utilizes a silica-based column to purify nucleic acids through centrifugation, providing rapid and efficient extraction. In contrast, the Magnetic Bead Method employs magnetic particles that bind nucleic acids, allowing for easy separation through magnetic fields. Both methods enhance the efficiency, accuracy, and reliability of nucleic acid extraction, catering to various applications in research, diagnostics, and clinical settings.</p></p>
