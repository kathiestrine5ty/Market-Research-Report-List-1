<p><p>The breast imaging technologies market is divided into two main types: ionizing and non-ionizing technologies. Ionizing breast imaging technologies, such as mammography, utilize X-rays to capture detailed images of breast tissue, aiding in early cancer detection. Non-ionizing breast imaging technologies include ultrasound and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), which use sound waves or magnetic fields, respectively, to produce images without harmful radiation exposure. Both categories offer critical diagnostic capabilities in breast health management, each with its unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
