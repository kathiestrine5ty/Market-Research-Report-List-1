<p><p>Modular construction is a building process that involves prefabricating sections of a structure in a factory setting before transporting them to the construction site for assembly. This approach enhances efficiency, reduces waste, and shortens project timelines, making it an attractive alternative to traditional construction methods. The Modular Construction Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.60% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for sustainable building practices, affordable housing solutions, and rapid urbanization.</p><p>Recent trends in the modular construction market include the adoption of advanced technologies such as Building Information Modeling (BIM) and 3D printing to improve design and manufacturing processes. Additionally, there is a growing focus on off-site construction due to labor shortages and rising material costs. The market is also witnessing an expansion in applications across various sectors, including residential, commercial, and healthcare, emphasizing flexibility and customization in building designs. Sustainability initiatives and stringent building regulations further contribute to the popularity of modular construction, positioning it as a key player in the future of the construction industry.</p></p>
