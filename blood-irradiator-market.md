<p><strong>The Blood Irradiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiator market is primarily segmented into two types: X-ray blood irradiation and gamma-ray blood irradiation. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to treat blood products, effectively reducing pathogens while preserving cellular integrity. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma radiation, commonly from isotopes like cobalt-60, to achieve similar microbial inactivation. Both methods enhance blood safety and duration of storage, playing critical roles in transfusion medicine by minimizing the risk of transfusion-transmitted infections.</p></p>
