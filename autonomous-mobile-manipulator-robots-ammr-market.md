<p><p>Autonomous Mobile Manipulator Robots (AMMR) are advanced robotic systems capable of navigating and performing tasks in various environments. The market is segmented into two main types: Omni-Directional Manipulators and Differential Manipulators. Omni-Directional Manipulators can move freely in any direction, offering superior maneuverability, making them ideal for dynamic environments. Differential Manipulators, on the other hand, utilize differential steering to navigate, providing stability and control, which is essential for tasks requiring precision. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries like logistics, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
