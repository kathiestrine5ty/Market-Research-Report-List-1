<p><ul><li>Solvent Cleaning</li><li>Hydrofluoric Acid Dip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Wafer Cleaning Chemicals market is primarily categorized into two types: solvent cleaning and hydrofluoric acid dip. Solvent cleaning involves using organic solvents to remove organic contaminants and residues from wafer surfaces, ensuring high cleanliness levels essential for semiconductor manufacturing. On the other hand, hydrofluoric acid dip specifically targets the removal of silicon dioxide layers, facilitating better adhesion for subsequent processing. Both methods are critical for maintaining the integrity and performance of silicon wafers in electronic applications.</p></p>
