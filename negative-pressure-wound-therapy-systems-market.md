<p><p>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) Systems are advanced medical devices used to promote wound healing by applying sub-atmospheric pressure to the surface of a wound. This therapy helps to draw out excess fluid, reduce edema, and promote tissue granulation, thereby accelerating the healing process. NPWT is commonly utilized in the management of acute and chronic wounds, including surgical wounds, diabetic foot ulcers, and pressure ulcers.</p><p>The Negative Pressure Wound Therapy Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period. Disposable NPWT systems, designed for single-use, offer convenience and reduce infection risk, making them ideal for acute care settings.
