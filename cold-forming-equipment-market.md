<p><p>Cold forming equipment varies by the number of dies used in the process, impacting production efficiency and complexity. A 1-die station is simple, often for low-volume production. The 2-die station improves efficiency with two operations per cycle. A 3-die station enhances capability for more intricate shapes. The 4-die and 5-die stations allow for greater automation and higher output, suitable for large-scale production. Other market types may include specialized or custom equipment, catering to unique manufacturing needs.</p></p>
