<p><strong>The Automotive Clutch Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-Type Clutch Bearing</li><li>Pull-Type Clutch Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive clutch bearing market includes two primary types: push-type and pull-type clutch bearings. Push-type clutch bearings are designed to engage the clutch when pressure is applied towards the transmission, commonly used in vehicles with hydraulic or cable-operated clutch systems. Pull-type clutch bearings, on the other hand, engage the clutch when tension is applied away from the transmission, often found in heavy-duty or high-performance vehicles. Both types ensure smooth clutch operation, reduce friction, and enhance vehicle performance, catering to different mechanical and operational requirements.</p></p>
