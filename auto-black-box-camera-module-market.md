<p><p>The auto black box camera module market primarily features two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors. CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for low-light conditions. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and are generally more cost-effective, which makes them popular in modern automotive applications. Both technologies play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
