<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Desalination System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Desalination Processes</li><li>Multi-stage Flash Distillation (MSF)</li><li>Multiple Effect Distillation (MED)</li><li>Vapour Compression Distillation (VCD)</li><li>Membrane Desalination Processes</li><li>Electrodialysis (ED)/Electrodialysis reversal (EDR)</li><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li><li>Ion exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desalination system market offers two primary types: thermal and membrane processes. Thermal methods include Multi-stage Flash Distillation (MSF), which evaporates seawater in stages; Multiple Effect Distillation (MED), utilizing sequential effects for efficiency; and Vapor Compression Distillation (VCD), which compresses vapor to produce fresh water. Membrane processes encompass Reverse Osmosis (RO), forcing water through semi-permeable membranes, and Electrodialysis (ED/EDR), which uses electrical currents to move ions through membranes. Additionally, ion exchange processes remove specific ions for water purification.</p></p>
