<p><p>Mine and IED detection systems can be classified into three main market types: sensor-based, radar-based, and laser-based systems. Sensor-based systems utilize ground-penetrating technology to identify explosive materials through soil analysis. Radar-based systems employ electromagnetic waves to detect concealed devices beneath the surface. Laser-based systems use focused light to assess and identify potential threats, offering high precision and minimal collateral damage. Each technology plays a crucial role in enhancing safety and effectiveness in detecting hidden explosives in various terrains.</p></p>
