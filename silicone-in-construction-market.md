<p><p>The silicone in construction market encompasses various types, including fluids, elastomers, resins, and gels. Fluids are used for lubrication and as sealants, providing moisture resistance. Elastomers offer flexibility and durability, making them ideal for joints and coatings. Resins are utilized in adhesives and sealants, enhancing structural integrity and bond strength. Gels serve applications in caulking and insulation, providing thermal and acoustic properties. Together, these silicone types enhance performance, sustainability, and longevity in construction projects.</p></p>
