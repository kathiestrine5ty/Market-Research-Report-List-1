<p><strong>The Reheating Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Walking Hearth Furnaces</li><li>Pusher Furnaces</li><li>Walking-Beam Furnaces</li><li>Refractory Beam Furnace</li><li>Tunnel Furnaces</li><li>Rotary Hearth Furnaces</li><li>Rototherm Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reheating furnaces market comprises several types used in metal processing. Walking hearth furnaces are characterized by a moving hearth that transports materials through the heating zone. Pusher furnaces employ a mechanism to push materials through the furnace, while walking-beam furnaces utilize beams to support and move items. Refractory beam furnaces leverage insulated beams, whereas tunnel furnaces offer continuous processing through a long chamber. Rotary hearth furnaces rotate material for uniform heating, and Rototherm furnaces provide efficient thermal management for diverse applications.</p></p>
