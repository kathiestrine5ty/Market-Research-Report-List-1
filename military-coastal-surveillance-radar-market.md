<p><p>The Military Coastal Surveillance Radar market is primarily divided into two types: X-Band and S-Band radar systems. X-Band radar, operating in the frequency range of 8 to 12 GHz, is ideal for high-resolution image capture and is effective in detecting small vessels and airborne threats in coastal areas. Conversely, S-Band radar, operating between 2 to 4 GHz, offers longer detection ranges and better performance in adverse weather conditions, making it suitable for broader surveillance applications. Both types enhance national security and maritime monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
