<p><ul><li>Flame Melting Method</li><li>Flux Method</li><li>Hydrothermal Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic ruby market comprises three primary methods: the Flame Melting Method, Flux Method, and Hydrothermal Method. The Flame Melting Method involves using high temperatures to melt aluminum oxide and create ruby crystals. The Flux Method utilizes a flux solution to facilitate crystal growth at lower temperatures, enhancing clarity and color. The Hydrothermal Method mimics natural formation conditions, using high pressure and temperature to grow rubies in a nutrient-rich solution. Each method affects quality, cost, and application in jewelry and industrial uses.</p></p>
