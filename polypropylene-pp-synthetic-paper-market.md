<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) synthetic paper is categorized into two main types: waterproof and non-waterproof. Waterproof PP synthetic paper is designed for applications that require resistance to water, making it ideal for outdoor use, such as signage, labels, and packaging that may be exposed to moisture. In contrast, non-waterproof PP synthetic paper is suitable for indoor applications where exposure to water is minimal. Both types offer durability, tear resistance, and printability, catering to various industries such as advertising, packaging, and labeling.</p></p>
