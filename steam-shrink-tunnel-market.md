<p><p>The Steam Shrink Tunnel market is categorized into Single Zone and Dual Zone Tunnels. Single Zone Tunnels utilize a single temperature zone to apply steam for shrink-wrapping products, making them ideal for smaller production lines. Dual Zone Tunnels, on the other hand, feature two separate temperature zones to optimize steam application and increase flexibility, accommodating a wider range of packaging materials and product types. This variety allows manufacturers to choose systems based on production capacity and packaging requirements.</p></p>
