<p><p>The HPA coated battery separator market is divided into two main types: wet diaphragm and dry diaphragm. Wet diaphragm separators are produced using a liquid-based process, providing enhanced electrochemical performance and high ionic conductivity, which is ideal for applications in lithium-ion batteries. In contrast, dry diaphragm separators are manufactured through a dry processing method, offering benefits such as lower production costs and improved mechanical strength, making them suitable for various battery types, including energy storage and electric vehicles.</p></p>
