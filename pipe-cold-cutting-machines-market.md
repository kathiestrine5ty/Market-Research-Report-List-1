<p><p>Pipe cold cutting machines are categorized based on their power sources: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. </p><p>Electric machines utilize electric motors, offering efficiency and ease of use in various environments. Pneumatic machines rely on compressed air, making them suitable for portable applications, often in tight or remote spaces. Hydraulic machines use fluid pressure for greater cutting force, ideal for heavy-duty tasks and thicker pipes. Each type caters to specific operational needs, preferences, and environmental conditions in the pipe cutting industry.</p></p>
