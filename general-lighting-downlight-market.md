<p><ul><li>Surface-mounted</li><li>Recessed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The General Lighting Downlight market is primarily categorized into two types: surface-mounted and recessed. Surface-mounted downlights are installed directly onto the ceiling, offering flexibility and ease of installation, making them ideal for spaces with varying ceiling heights. Recessed downlights, on the other hand, are embedded into the ceiling, providing a sleek, streamlined appearance that allows for even distribution of light while maximizing floor space. Both types are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications for their aesthetic appeal and functionality.</p></p>
