<p><p>Micro Electro Mechanical System (MEMS) based accelerometers measure acceleration and tilt in various applications. They come in different types based on their axis sensitivity. A 1-axis accelerometer detects motion in a single direction, ideal for basic applications. The 2-axis accelerometer measures in two directions, suited for applications requiring directional awareness. A 3-axis accelerometer captures movement across all three spatial dimensions, making it versatile for complex applications. Combo accelerometers integrate multiple sensors, providing enhanced functionality and capabilities for advanced systems.</p></p>
