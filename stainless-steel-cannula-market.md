<p><p>The stainless steel cannula market is segmented into two main types: blunt cannula and deflected cannula. Blunt cannulas are characterized by their rounded tip, minimizing tissue damage and reducing the risk of accidental punctures, making them suitable for sensitive procedures. Deflected cannulas feature a curved design, allowing for enhanced maneuverability in challenging anatomical regions, which is beneficial in procedures requiring precision. Both types are essential in medical applications, contributing to improved patient safety and procedural effectiveness.</p></p>
