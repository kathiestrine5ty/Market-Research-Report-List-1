<p><p>The cryotherapy devices market comprises three primary types: Cryosurgery Devices, Localized Cryotherapy Devices, and Cryochambers. Cryosurgery Devices are used in medical settings to treat various conditions by applying extreme cold to targeted areas, effectively destroying abnormal tissues. Localized Cryotherapy Devices focus on specific body parts to alleviate pain and inflammation, commonly used in sports medicine. Cryochambers, on the other hand, provide whole-body exposure to cryogenic temperatures, promoting recovery, wellness, and overall health benefits for users.</p></p>
